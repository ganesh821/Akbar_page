K V Hidayathulla

Founder
President & Director

Leader of Akbar Group since 1990, pioneered over 100 offices pan India.

Mr. Hidayathulla has been the Group CEO for Akbar Group since 1990. He is man of immense skillsets in business and people management. Riding on his leadership skills and efficient networking, Akbar group has grown nationwide to more than 100 offices Pan India – a mercurial success.

All the airline accolades that he has been awarded are a proof of his contribution in creating a unique global platform for Client-vendor Engagement Facilitation. He acquired ARC credentials from USA for Akbar Group. He has dedicated his time, skills and talent in expanding Akbar Group’s hospitality and tourism division from the moment he graduated from Madurai Kamaraj University. The new travel vertical of Akbar Group- Akbar Offshore Pvt. Ltd. that includes Holiday division of Akbar Group, sprouted on March 27, 2019 - his vision come true. As a President and Director of Akbar Offshore Pvt. Ltd, he has played a vital role in consolidating airlines on Akbar Platform. His uplifting, influential and dedicated demeanour drives everyone in the office and he’s a majestic motivator to have around.
